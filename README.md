# Domino’s Sales Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard built using sales data sourced from Excel.
It analyzes revenue, orders, quantity sold, city performance, product categories, and payment methods to help understand business performance and support data-driven decisions.

## Tech Stack

* Data Source: Microsoft Excel
* Data Visualization: Power BI
* Power BI Features Used:
* Power Query (data cleaning & transformation)
* DAX measures
* Interactive visuals
* Slicers & filters
* KPI cards

## Features

* KPI cards for Total Revenue, Total Orders, and Total Quantity
* Quarter-wise slicer (Q1–Q4) for dynamic filtering
* Monthly & quarterly revenue trend analysis
* City-wise revenue comparison
* Category-wise quantity analysis (Pizza, Sides, Beverage, Dessert)
* Payment method distribution (Cash, UPI, Card)
* User-friendly and business-focused dashboard design

## Goal of the Dashboard

The main goal of this dashboard is to:
  * Convert raw Excel data into meaningful insights
  * Track sales performance across time and locations
  * Identify top-selling products and cities
  * Support management in making data-driven business decisions

## Key Insights

* Mumbai and Pune are the highest revenue-generating cities
* Pizza contributes the highest quantity sold
* Digital payments (UPI & Card) dominate over cash
* Sales show strong performance during Q2 (April–June)
* Delhi and Bangalore show opportunities for growth

## Business Impact

* Helps stakeholders monitor sales performance at a glance
* Improves decision-making for city-wise and product-wise strategies
* Supports inventory and demand planning
* Encourages focus on high-performing payment methods
* Reduces manual reporting effort through automation
